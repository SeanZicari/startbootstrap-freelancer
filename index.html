Procast
Recording — Mixing — Mastering — Syndication

We are a recording, mixing, mastering & syndication service for podcasters. Whether you are just starting out or already have a podcast, we can help!

 

The Process

{#-
Planning
-#}

We'll plan & prepare for your podcast. We will also provide a recommended equipment list, if needed, to get you started.

{#-
Recording
-#}

You'll record your podcast with us. Then, we'll mix & master it. We'll send it to you for approval or revisions. We'll do this until you're satisfied with the result.

{#-
Podcast!
-#}

We'll create an RSS file and syndicate your podcast. We'll host everything for you, or you can host it yourself.

About

Procast exists to help you share your passion with an eager listening audience. There are thousands of people out there who want to know more about you and what interests you.

We can turn your raw ideas into a broadcast-quality podcast that people will want to subscribe to. You create the content, and we'll create the podcast!
